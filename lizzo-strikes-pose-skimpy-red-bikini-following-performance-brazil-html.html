Lizzo strikes a pose in a skimpy red bikini following her first-ever performance in Brazil

Lizzo was a sight to behold as she dominated the stage during her performance at the YouTube Space in Rio on Friday morning. 

And by the afternoon, the 31-year-old once again called for all eyes on her as she posed in a skimpy red bikini while soaking up the Copacabana sun. 

'Roll Model,' the Truth Hurts hit maker - who performed for the first time ever in the Portuguese speaking country - jokingly captioned the photos.

Confidence: Lizzo shared a slew of bikini-clad snapshots from her beach day in Copacabana, Rio de Janeiro to her Instagram page on Friday

Cheeky: The 31-year-old singer oozed confidence in a skimpy red bikini as she immersed herself in the waves

With her hair blowing about in the beach breeze, the singer oozed confidence as the waves crashed against her ankles.

She gave fans a view of her derriere in one shot, while she struck a sassy pose with her hand on her hip in another.

Hairstylist Shelby Swain joined the singer in one snapshot where they can be seen playing about in the rolling waves.

Lizzo has been in Brazil since Thursday in anticipation of her first-ever Brazilian performance, which took place Friday morning.

For her debut, the Grammy Award winner slipped her curvaceous figure into a unique lilac two piece.

The silky set included a crop-top that featured a flattering sweetheart neckline, along with a pair of wide legged pants with suspenders that hung at the singer's sides.

Lizzo was all smiles as she belted out some of her most memorable hits for eager Brazilian-based fans. 

Taking the stage: The singer belted out some of her biggest hits during her YouTube Space performance in Rio 

All smiles: Lizzo could not help but beam as she handed the mic off to the crowd of Brazilian fans

She sported a fun shade of aqua and yellow eye shadow at the event, which were evocative of the Brazilian flag.

Lizzo also wore a different look to the event, a 'Black Renaissance' printed ruched one-shoulder midi dress designed by PrettyLittleThing. 

As if the singer was not dealing with enough excitement surrounding her career, she found out shortly after her performance that her March 13th show at the Houston Rodeo had sold out in merely seven minutes.

Lizzo, in a cloud of expletives and thankful sentiments, took to Instagram to give her perspective on the impressive feat.

Good as Hell: The singer looked gorgeous in a figure hugging dress by PrettyLittleThing

Sold out! Shortly after her performance, Lizzo learned that her March 13th Houston Rodeo show had sold out in seven minutes, prompting her to head over to Instagram to thank fans for their support

'God is good! I want to sat thank you to everybody who bought tickets to the Houston Rodeo. It's about to be a party, b----! I love y'all!' she exclaimed in her Instagram story. 

Earlier this week, the singer and flutist uploaded a different video to her Instagram feed to announce the Texas show, in which she was decked out in western-style duds while riding a mechanical bull a la Debra Winger in Urban Cowboy.

And late last month, Lizzo, born Melissa Viviane Jefferson, won three of the eight Grammys for which she was nominated this year, for Best Pop Solo Performance for her smash sleeper hit Truth Hurts, Best Traditional R&B Performance for her track Jerome, and Best Urban Contemporary Album for Cuz I Love You, the deluxe edition.

She lost out, however, on the three 'top' trophies of the evening — Song of the Year, Record of the Year and Album of the Year — to 18-year-old Billie Eilish, who also clinched the honor of Best New Artist.
