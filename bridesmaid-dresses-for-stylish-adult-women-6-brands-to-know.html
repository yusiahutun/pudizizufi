Bridesmaid brands for stylish women

We earn a commission for products purchased through some links in this article.

Regan Cameron for Harper's Bazaar

Self-Portrait

SHOP SELF-PORTRAIT

Every fashionable celebrity, from the Duchess of Sussex and the Princess of Wales to Alexa Chung, is a fan of Self-Portrait. The brand is known for its use of lace, colour, and print – and its ladylike designs are perfect for a wedding day, whether you're a guest, bride or bridesmaid. Self-Portrait is praised for its timeless approach to fashion, meaning your bridesmaids will love their dresses for years to come.

Sleeper

SHOP SLEEPER

Sleeper was launched in 2014 by two former fashion editors, Kate Zubarieva and Asya Varetsa, after one of them had a dream about standing in the middle of a pyjama factory. Their first collection debuted in the following winter and ever since then, the label has been adored by the fashion industry and beyond. Sleeper's recent bridal collection features the perfect candidate for a bridesmaid dress, as do many of the label's other designs. We love this fun take on the classic slip with a feather trim.

Cecilie Bahnsen

SHOP CECILIE BAHNSEN

Owning a Cecilie Bahnsen dress is like owning a piece of art. Each garment sparks so much joy, whether it's felt by the wearer or those who spot you wearing it. The most beautiful thing about Bahnsen's designs is the fact that you can wear them for a special occasion, like a wedding, but also, far beyond.

"You could put one of our dresses on and wear it to a party, but they are not just for these occasions, they are also just for the enjoyment of life, for dressing up every single day. This is what inspires me. It is dressing up just for living,” the designer previously told Bazaar.

Rixo

SHOP RIXO

Rixo offers easy-to-wear styles in timeless silhouettes that flatter every shape, ideal for bridesmaid shopping, as it's unrealistic to expect every different shape or size to feel comfortable in the same style. Browse from Rixo's extensive range of styles, shapes and patterns to ensure you're feeling as confident as possible on the big day – and make sure to check out the brand's special bridesmaid range too.

Maggie Marilyn

SHOP MAGGIE MARILYN

Sustainable designer Maggie Hewitt creates some of the most effortlessly elegant dresses you will find, many of which will make perfect bridesmaid dresses. The New Zealand-based brand is all about transparency when it comes to its designs, and anything bought for a wedding day would make a great investment for life. "I think it’s becoming increasingly important to know who is making our clothes," Hewitt previously told us. "They are as much a part of our lives as we are of theirs."

Vince

SHOP VINCE

Inspired by the founder's hometown of Los Angeles, Vince offers elegant and timeless dresses for the chicest of weddings. With pieces made from luxury fabrics, the brand focuses on intricate detailing and design that's built to last, meaning you'll be the most stylish bridesmaid around in one of their silk pieces.

Reformation

SHOP REFORMATION

Head over to Reformation and be prepared for a true test of willpower – every look is so expertly styled you'll want everything. While its offering goes beyond bridalwear, the wedding and occasionwear category serves up plenty to choose from. From mini dresses to long-sleeve dresses, there's something for all tastes and shapes.

Ghost

SHOP GHOST

A long-term frontrunner in the chic bridesmaid dress arena, Ghost's feminine gowns are characterised by their signature bias-cut, form-fitting, fluid silhouettes, as well as the brand's garment-dyeing process which results in a vintage-satin effect.

The Vampire's Wife

SHOP THE VAMPIRE'S WIFE

For some, the idea of adult women all wearing the same dress, regardless of differing shapes and personalities, feels a little antiquated. The Vampire's Wife creates beautifully romantic dresses in different materials, colours and silhouettes that work perfectly all together. The brand offers uniformity without negating the personalities of the wearers.

Galvan

SHOP GALVAN

Season after season, fashion editors and influencers head to Galvan for chic pieces that tap into trends, without falling victim to them. It's not a label that's bridal-focused, but you will find a lot of strong options in its dresses and jumpsuit sections that will be perfect for walking your best friend up the aisle.

Needle & Thread

SHOP NEEDLE & THREAD

Needle & Thread's intricate embroidery and beautiful tulle shapes have made it a favourite for bridesmaids who want to wear something special, feminine and modern. If lace, ornate floral detailing and sequins are your game, then this is the bridesmaid brand for you. Think ballerina meets bohemia.

Rewritten

SHOP REWRITTEN

Just as its name suggests, Rewritten aims to rewrite the rules of traditional wedding attire. Choose between 10 existing styles, each available in a number of different colours and with UK sizes from eight to 30. Either you and your fellow bridesmaids could opt for the same shade but a different style to best suit your shapes, or the same style but in varying colours to work best with different skin tones. The point is inclusivity and designing dresses that will work hard for you beyond the wedding. If you're unlikely to wear a floor-length option more than once, then Rewritten will happily shorten it so you get more mileage.

Grace Loves Lace

SHOP GRACE LOVES LACE

Grace Loves Lace counts romantic bohemia as its signature. The Australian label is famed for its French lace, relaxed shapes and easy timelessness. Expect a lot of bare shoulders, pastel shades, scalloped hems and floaty sleeves.

Vanessa Cocchiaro​

SHOP VANESSA COCCHIARO

Vanessa Cocchiaro strongly believes that a bride's closest women should be viewed as 'heroines' in their own right. With a mix of separates, dresses and jumpsuits, the brand is a go-to for subtle, chic and feminine looks.
