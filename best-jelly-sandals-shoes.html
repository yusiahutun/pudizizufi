The 15 Best Jelly Sandals Shoes for Your Summer Wardrobe 2024

Best Sustainable Jelly Sandals

Melissa Possession Jelly Fisherman Sandal

Best Sustainable Jelly Sandals

Melissa Possession Jelly Fisherman Sandal

Credit: Nordstrom

If your goal is to shop ethically, try Melissa’s collection of jellies. The brand has been around for decades and recently made hugely eco-conscious strides by creating 100 percent recyclable products. A favorite is this pair of jelly sandals in a grape hue, a confection of fruit-scented PVC.

Sizes: 5–10

Colors: Purple, blue, orange, pink

What reviewers are saying: “Cute and comfortable. Going to get this in other colors.”

Stuart Weitzman SW Bow Slides

Most Versatile Jelly Sandals

Stuart Weitzman SW Bow Slides

Best Chunky-Heel Jelly Sandals

Gucci Rubber Slide Sandal

Credit: gucci.com

When you think of a typical jelly sandal, a shimmery PVC pair might come to mind. But there are so many other versions to consider, like these chunky ’90s-era Gucci sandals.
