What kinds of oils are best for the face?

.

According to Marino, a few low-comedogenic ingredients to look for include jojoba oil, marula oil, squalane, rosehip seed oil, sea buckthorn oil, black currant seed oil, and olive oil.

Which oils are best for anti-aging?

"Antioxidant-rich oils are going to be the best for anti-aging because they fight off the free radical damage that breaks down the skin, causing wrinkles, pigmentation and other aging concerns," Marino notes.

Will using a face oil cause clogged pores?

Using one of the best facial oils—primarily those that are non-comedogenic—should not clog pores, but Marino advises regular skin exfoliation prior to application.

"It’s important to remember that exfoliation plays a key role in the skin's overall health and appearance," she says. If the skin is not properly exfoliated, congestion is likely to build up within the pore when you add hydrating or moisturizing ingredients like oils. The dead cells and the oil will stick together forming a glue-like substance, creating a layer which will actually prevent moisture retention, and may lead to breakouts."
