The VERY glamorous UFC WAGS on the arms of some of the world's toughest fighters

It is a brutal sport that requires immense skill, years of sacrifice and a great deal of bravery.

Second in difficulty to being inside the octagon itself is most likely having to watch your loved one put their body on the line in front of a baying crowd.

But the most famous stars of the Ultimate Fighting Championship, more commonly known as the UFC, live very different lives with their partners away from the bright lights of fight night.


While some fighters are in long-term relationships with their childhood sweetheart, others are currently dating ring girls or have found love with female fighters in the sport.

MailOnline has taken a look at some of the rocks behind the rock-hard MMA fighters on the world's biggest stage.

Dee Devlin, fiancé of Conor McGregor

Dee Devlin pictured at the Chopard Loves Cinema dinner at the 75th Cannes Film Festival in France in May last year

Dee pictured the screening of Elvis during the 75th annual Cannes film festival at Palais des Festivals on May 25, 2022

The couple first met back in 2008 when Conor McGregor was 19 and Dee was 20, and are now engaged

Dee and Conor attend the Chopard Gentleman's Evening during the 75th annual Cannes film festival at Rooftop Hotel Martinez

The partner of the man who is notorious both in name and nature, Dee Devlin and Conor McGregor have been loved up since well before the Irishman shot to fame.

The couple met back in 2008 when Conor was 19 and Dee was 20.

The fighter had just quit his job as a plumbing apprentice and was struggling to make ends meet as he tried to pursue his passion, with Dee standing by him as he forged his career in the MMA industry. 

They now share three children together - Conor Jr, Croia and Rían, and they got engaged in 2020 after 12 years of dating. 

McGregor told The Sun: 'She has helped me throughout this career. If it wasn’t for her, I probably wouldn’t be where I am today.'

Vitor Belfort made his UFC debut aged just 19 before rising to prominence and being now tied for third for the most finishes, with 14 knockout victories to his name.

He is married to Brazilian-American Joana Prado, with whom he has three children.

The former actress and model has featured in Playboy magazine on multiple occasions and now runs a series of gyms and fitness brands.

The couple met in 2000, initially splitting before rekindling their romance  and marrying in 2003.

Retired UFC star Chael Sonnen fought in a number of MMA promotions during a career that started back in 1997. But it was a chance encounter that led to him meeting the love of his life.

After two years of dating, the couple became married in July 2013. They have a son and daughter together named Thero and Pria. Their third child Blauna Dian tragically died shortly after birth. 

Speaking on the MMA Hour with Ariel Helwani, Sonnen said: 'I met her at a flight. She was passing through. It was a three-story building. I saw on the bottom floor and she got away. 

'And I searched the whole building for her and I found her on the middle floor. I went up to her, and I just said my phone is dead. I've got to make a phone call.

'She loaned me her phone. I called myself. Oh, my phone wasn’t dead. I felt it vibrate, and I handed her phone back and then I text her instantly and said careful who you lose your phone to a lo. Had the number, took her out the next night and told her "I’m gonna marry you".'

'As a nurse and as his wife, God forbid he’d have to go to the hospital there. It’s a very different system.'
