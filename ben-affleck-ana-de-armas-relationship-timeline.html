Ben Affleck &amp; Ana de Armas Relationship Timeline, Dating Rumors

Another day, another Hollywood couple that makes you go "… Really?" In 2020, we welcomed Ben Affleck, 47, and Ana de Armas, 31, to the club. The duo were speculated to be romantically linked since they were spotted vacationing together in Havana, Cuba, in March 2020. However, January 2021 brought bad news for fans of the couple.

Here's everything we know about their relationship so far.

January 2021: The couple split after almost a year together.

All good things must come to an end, and it would appear that Ana de Armas and Ben Affleck's budding romance is over. People reported the sad news, with a source revealing, "Ben is no longer dating Ana ... She broke it off. Their relationship was complicated." People's source also explained, "Ana doesn't want to be Los Angeles based and Ben obviously has to since his kids live in Los Angeles."

While de Armas and Affleck have hit pause on their relationship for now, the split was reportedly amicable. "This is something that was mutual and something that is completely amicable," the source said. "They are in different points in their lives; there is deep love and respect there. Ben continues to want to work on himself. He has three jobs lined up and he’s a solid father at home. They are both happy with where they are in their lives."

December 2020: Ben and Ana move in together.

It looks like de Armas and Affleck have taken their relationship to the next level. After going public with their romance earlier this year, it's being reported that de Armas has moved into Affleck's home in Los Angeles. A source confirmed the move to People, and said that de Armas had listed her Venice, California home for sale.

A "friend" also told the publication that Affleck and de Armas's "relationship continues to be strong."

November 28, 2020: Ben and Ana go on a park date with his kids.

The couple was spotted taking Affleck's kids to a Santa Monica park after spending Thanksgiving together.

De Armas wore an all-pale-pink outfit, pairing a voluminous maxi skirt with a cami and finishing off the look with a gray sweater tied around her shoulders. Meanwhile, Affleck went down the monochromatic route, too, sporting all-gray with his blazer, sweater, and trousers.

November 2020: Ben and Ana spend their first Thanksgiving together.

Having returned to the set of Deep Water in New Orleans earlier this month, Affleck and de Armas "enjoyed a family-filled Thanksgiving weekend together" in Los Angeles, per People. According to the publication, the happy couple hosted Affleck's children—Violet, Seraphina, and Samuel—who the actor shares with his ex-wife, Jennifer Garner.

A source told the publication, "Ben and Ana seem great ... Everyone seemed very happy. Ana has been traveling so she had not seen the kids for a while."

October 15, 2020: After months of staying relatively low-key, Ana and Ben return to the public eye.

Much has changed since we last saw Affleck and de Armas: The president got COVID-19, Nicki Minaj gave birth, and SZA even released new music. Sightings of Affleck, who has been busy filming The Last Duel in Ireland, and de Armas had abruptly stopped after months of PDA-filled appearances.

Now, however, the couple has returned.

They were photographed outside the actor's Pacific Palisades home Thursday, with Affleck greeting his beau at the door while wearing a T-shirt that read, "Asere ya, gracias." As for de Armas, she wore a babydoll dress and carried a large black shoulder bag.

De Armas has also reportedly been spending more time with Affleck and his kids, whom he shares with his ex-wife, Jennifer Garner.

August 18, 2020: Ana helps Ben stay sober.

Affleck has battled an addiction to alcohol for years. According to a source for E! News, de Armas is very supportive of him in keeping his sobriety.

"Since Ana has been in Ben's life, being sober has become easier to manage," the source said. "She came into his life and has given him everything he wants and needs in a partner. He's very satisfied with Ana and isn't looking for a coping method. She has a vivacious personality and is a very talented actress."

Luckily, the two have similar lifestyles when it comes to alcohol. "Drinking isn't part of the equation with Ana," the source continued. "She's very supportive and she doesn't party or make alcohol a big part of her life. It's not what they do as a couple or a part of their relationship. He really hasn't felt the want or need since they have gotten into a relationship."

De Armas has also apparently cultivated a good relationship with his kids, whom he shares with ex-wife Jennifer Garner. "She's wonderful with his kids and very loving. It's hard to put into words how happy he is with her. She fills him up and he doesn't need anything else," the insider said.

Ultimately, Affleck seems to be content with de Armas. "He's working out a lot and feeling great," another insider told the outlet. "He continues to focus on his family and his new relationship. It's been a great few months for them as a couple."
