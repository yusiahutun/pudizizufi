7 Best Moisture-Wicking Bras to Prevent Sweaty Boobs

Moisture-wicking bras are the name and defeating sweaty undergarments is the game. No matter your cup size, we can all relate to that icky feeling of a clammy chest on a hot summer day. The good news is, there are actually breathable bras out there that are equipped to absorb moisture from our bodies and cool us down. 

Finding the best bra that will keep you cool and comfy depends on your needs. However, keeping in mind that lightly padded bras made of moisture-wicking fabrics like cotton and bamboo will serve you well in almost all scenarios. 

Not sure where to start? Keep scrolling to shop FIRST‘s picks for the best moisture-wicking bras so you can survive sweaty bra season like a pro.
