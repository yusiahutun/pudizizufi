10. Floyd Mayweather Jr.

Floyd Mayweather Jr. is an American boxing promoter and former professional boxer. He competed between 1996 and 2017 and retired undefeated, winning 15 major world championships, from super featherweight to light middleweight.

Floyd has a $24 million Miami Beach mansion, making him one of the biggest male celebrities who live in Miami. The 10,853-square-foot South Coconut Lane home spans three stories and covers a massive 5,000-square-foot rooftop deck.

13. Cristina Saralegui
